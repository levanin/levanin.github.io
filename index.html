About Me

Raised in South Africa, living in New Zealand. Working on a PhD in Post Quantum Cryptography at University of Auckland in 2022 under Steven Galbraith. I love Muay Thai & Mathematics. My mathematical interests include number theory, combinatorics, algebraic graph theory.

Current Projects

Applying zero-knowledge proof systems (such as zkSNARKs) to obtain faster and smaller post-quantum protocols.
