NEW: Looking for Work!

I have submitted my PhD thesis as of April 30 2025 and I am looking for research positions in central/western Europe from mid-September 2025. My preference would be for an industry position, but I am open to postdoctoral roles as well. I am primarily interested in constructing practical, provably secure protocols; both in the post-quantum (isogeny and code-based) and classical (discrete-log-based) setting. My primary focus in my PhD thesis has been applying modern, succinct generic proof systems (zk-SNARKs) as a building block for efficient post-quantum protocols, such as verifiable random functions, signature schemes and more.
